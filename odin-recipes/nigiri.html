Nigiri

Description

Make your own Nigiri at home! It's super easy and delicious.

Ingredients

  • Sushi Rice
  • Sushi Vinegar
  • Sushi Grade Fish

Steps

  1. Cook Sushi Rice according to insructions of the bag
  2. Add sushi vinegar to the rice and let it cool
  3. Cut Fish into thin slices
  4. Form a small oblong ball with the rice
  5. Lay the Fish on the rice and squeeze
  6. Enjoy!
