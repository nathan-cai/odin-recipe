Sushi

Description

A simple, easy, and delicious recipe for making salmon sushi. Try it out!

Ingredients

  • Sushi Rice
  • Sushi Vinegar
  • Nori
  • Sushi Grade Salmon
  • Avacado

Steps

  1. Cook Sushi Rice according to insructions of the bag
  2. Add sushi vinegar to the rice and let it cool
  3. Spread a thin layer of rice over the nori
  4. cut the salmon and avacado into thin strips
  5. Lay the salmon and avocado strips across the rice
  6. Roll up the nori
  7. Cut into slices
  8. Enjoy!
